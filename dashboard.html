Insights from PCA

This section shows the insights obtained through Principal Component Analysis (PCA).

PCA Preparations

Columns that were attempted to use: OutputYear, ProjectStatus, ProjectRDC.

However, when One Hot Encoding was applied, we found that there were too many categories.

This means some small enough principal components were needed to represent the original data.
